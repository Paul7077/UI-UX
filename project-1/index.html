Virat Crane Industries

Where Purity
Meets taste

Virat Crane Industries Ltd is proud to bring you 'Durga Ghee,' a brand synonymous with trust, tradition, and quality. Since becoming part of the Crane Group in February 1995, under the leadership of Shri G.V.S.L. Kantha Rao, our current Managing Director, the Durga brand has become a household name, known for its unmatched standards and reliability. We are committed to delivering excellence through a range of premium dairy products, including Kamadenu Cow Ghee, Durga Ghee, Durga Milk, and Durga Curd. Every product we create is crafted with the utmost care in a hygienic environment using only pure, high-quality ingredients. Free from adulteration and 100% safe for consumption, our products reflect our promise of authenticity and health.
